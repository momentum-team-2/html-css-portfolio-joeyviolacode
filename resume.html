Freelance Musician - Viola/Violin Performance

For the past 16 years, I've been a musician freelancing throughout the southeast. My focus has been on Baroque performance practice, but my experience ranges widely outside of that. I've been a principal and section player for the North Carolina Baroque Orchestra since its inception in 2012, after having performed in countless performances that served to biuld an audience for Baroque music in the Carolinas and beyond, including regular performances on Bach Cantata series at Duke Chapel and in many performances with the Duke Vespers and Evensong ensembles. In addition to my work in Baroque music, I have played in pit orchestras and traditional dance bands throughout the area.
